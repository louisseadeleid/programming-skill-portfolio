Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It is creamy, delicious, and easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta (or bacon), diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente, then drain, reserving some pasta water.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and cook the pancetta until crispy. Add minced garlic and saut√© briefly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cheese, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Remove the pan from heat and quickly toss the hot spaghetti with the pancetta.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water if needed.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top.
