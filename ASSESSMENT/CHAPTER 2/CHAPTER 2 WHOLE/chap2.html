The Summer I Turned Pretty

Overview

The Summer I Turned Pretty is a coming-of-age romantic drama series based on the novel by Jenny Han. The story follows Belly Conklin as she navigates love, friendships, and self-discovery during summers spent at Cousins Beach.

The series captures themes of family, first love, and personal growth, making it a nostalgic and heartwarming watch.

Cast & Crew

Creator: Jenny Han

Main Cast:

  • Lola Tung as Belly Conklin
  • Christopher Briney as Conrad Fisher
  • Gavin Casalegno as Jeremiah Fisher
  • Jackie Chung as Laurel
  • Rachel Blanchard as Susannah

Characters

Belly Conklin: The protagonist who experiences love and self-discovery during the summer.

Conrad Fisher: The older Fisher brother, mysterious and brooding.

Jeremiah Fisher: The younger Fisher brother, charismatic and fun-loving.

Laurel: Belly's mother, a writer and a close friend of Susannah.

Susannah: The Fisher boys’ mother and a beloved figure in Belly's life.
